Our Values

Everything we do, we do by heart and follow our values.

Values

Individual Independence:

Changes in personal capabilities are usually not monitored or recorded until it is too late and diagnose has been given. For many people this means having to depend on others to get through the day and removes the presence of their Individual Independence.
With the assistance of our services, it will be possible to detect functional and memory disorders as early as possible and take preventive actions. In unity with our customers, we help restoring independence as well as improving the daily life.

Digital Dignity:

We are operating with a high sense of Digital Dignity, providing secure storage of life data. You own your life data and have a full control over it.
Respecting privacy and data ethics
New technology and excessive data collection can seem intimidating and foreign to people. Our Platform incorporates MyData tools which bring a person back in control of the data that is gathered and generated about her. Individuals will be able to have a better presence in the digital environment by owning their life data.
Our Platform is private and secure by design and default. Our services are built upon the principles of ethics and trust and we rigorously follow them.

Valuable Life:

Understanding your way of living and its patterns is the key to a Valuable Life and to making changes for better. We provide you with knowledge about your life, which goes far beyond basic physical measurements. Even small adjustments to daily routines can help prevent unfavourable outcomes and improve your quality of life.
