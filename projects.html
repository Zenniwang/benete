Project

BeWell Happy

We have participated in BeWell Happy research project held by the partner network during 2014-2016. The project focused on the recognition of changes in functional and cognitive capabilities of elderly living at home.

Supporing eldery’s life

The main goal was to develop common-good business model of how technology can improve elderly’s quality of life and increase their independence in the living environment.
Another important objective was to study whether ambient technology can be used to detect dementia or changes in capabilities that can indicate the onset of cognitive decline. During the research stage, participants’ daily activities and changes in them were monitored with the help of ambient sensor technology.
Family members had unlimited opportunity to follow the elderly health status based on their daily performance.
Founding from the BeWell project stressed that data- and information-based solutions can support elderly’s daily performance while improving the overall quality of life.
